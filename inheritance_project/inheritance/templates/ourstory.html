OUR STORY

VISION

Inheritance needs you to seriously consider the question - central to which is the question of humanity. Humanity is the story of mankind. It includes every man, woman, and child born since the first human couple. It includes those not born and still no less valued to someone, somewhere, for their reasons. Humanity is a chain of events - random and not, contributions by others - implicit and explicit, whether in truth or by perception unrelated to the naked eye, bringing together people born apart. To connect in harmony, possibly for a lifetime. Humanity is the story of unpredictable efforts that can and at times do change the future, forever. Everyone makes a contribution to the story of humanity, our universe - inspiring us all for a moment or lifetime.
