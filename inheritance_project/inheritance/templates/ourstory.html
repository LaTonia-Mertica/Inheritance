VISION

Humanity is the story of mankind. It includes every man, woman, and child born since the first human couple. It includes those not born yet, who no less will be valued to someone, somewhere. It is a chain of random events. The contributions made by others. Some are invisible to the naked eye. The selfish efforts of a few have sometimes altered destinies, forever. Whether we know it, or not, everyone makes a contribution to the story of humanity, and consequently, our universe.
