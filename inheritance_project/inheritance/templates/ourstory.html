VISION

Humanity is the story of mankind. It includes every man, woman, and child born since the first human couple. It includes those not born yet, who no less will be valued to someone, somewhere. It is a chain of random events. The contributions made by others. Some are invisible to the naked eye. Dozens of coincidental acts by others that result in bringing together people born miles apart, even worlds apart. Despite the odds they manage to connect in blissful harmony. Often, for a lifetime. The selfish efforts of a few that have altered destinies, forever. Whether we admit it, or not, everyone makes a contribution to the story of humanity, and consequently, our universe. We have all been inspired by these actions, or by countless objects, for a moment, or for our entire lives. So... WHAT MAKES YOU ADD TO THE STORY?
